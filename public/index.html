Supply concentration (fetched cohort)

Each row’s % of circulating supply comes from the API. The bars are running sums of those percentages for the top k ranks (after your sort). Anything not in your fetched list is simply the rest of circulation—the same idea as comparing the token’s circulating total to the balances you see in the table.
